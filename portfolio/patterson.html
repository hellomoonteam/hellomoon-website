Patterson Dental

Audience Response Game

We love games. We love playing them, and we love making them. This space-themed trivia game broke Patterson Dental's audience of trainees into five teams to see who had the winning combination of Modern Dental/Kitschy Space knowledge.

Audience Response

Each trainee is given an audience response pad with a color that matches one of the five teams. As a series of questions are displayed, players choose the corresponding answer on their pads, eliciting groans or cheers as scores are tallied.
