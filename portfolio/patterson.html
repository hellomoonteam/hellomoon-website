Audience Response Game

One of the most effective ways of interacting with an audience is by playing games. This space themed game allowed Patterson Dental training attendees to break into teams and compete to see who had the winning combination of Modern Dental / Kitchy Space knowledge.

Audience Response

Each attendee is given an audience response pad with a color matching one of five teams. As a series of questions are displayed players choose the corresponding answer on their pad illiciting groans and cheers as the score for each question are tallied.
