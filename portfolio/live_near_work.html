Live Near Your Work

Community Website

We built the "Live Near Your Work" site to highlight the character and unique qualities of each of the four distinct neighborhoods encircling the University of Minnesota in Minneapolis. Featuring succinct neighborhood highlights, a series of video interviews with local residents and a property listing system it's a great place to learn about the area and the benefits of a walkable lifestyle.
