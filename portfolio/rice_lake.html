Rice Lake

Product Interface Design

An Interface For Everything

Rice Lake makes industrial scales. The control systems for these scales, called indicators, are extremely powerful and configurable. They're used in a variety of applications, from weighing luggage at the airport to controlling entire food processing factories.

When they began working on a new series of indicators we were brought in to analyze the current product line, interview users to assess needs and ultimately design a bullet proof interface that can gracefully handle a diverse array of applications.

Configurable

We designed a wide array of components that can be used to layout a scale display specifically tailored to each users application. Need to track your chocolate chip cookie factoy's hoppers? We've got an icon for that.
