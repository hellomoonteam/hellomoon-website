The Commons

Education App

The Commons is an interactive learning app designed to teach kids about the effects we have on our shared environments. One of our favorite parts of the project was a game we created allowing two students to compete to see who can make the most money grazing cattle on a shared pasture. Put too many cows on your pasture too fast and you'll destroy the resource but if you're patient and let your small herd thrive you'll come out ahead.

Guided Learning

Students play games, watch videos and try out simulations to learn about the commons. At the conclusion of each activity they're shown a summary of what's just occurred and asked to make observations.

Tracking Progress

The experience is broken into four distinct lessons and student progress is tracked and displayed in an easy to understand navigation drawer.

Lots of Math

We're usually pretty comfortable with math but to be on the safe side we brought in a math teacher from the University of Minnesota to check our work on some of the trickier formulas.
