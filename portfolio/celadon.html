Celadon Managed Wealth

Website

When Celadon Managed Wealth came to us the first thing we said was - great logo! Celadon is a pale green color found in ancient Chinese ceramics and American dollar bills. The logo uses cross hatching and patterns similar to those found in currency. We continued this theme by creating complimentary patterns and illustrations to use throughout the site we built.
