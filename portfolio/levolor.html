Levolor

Product Configuration

The Perfect Way to the Perfect Shade

Levolor sells custom window blinds. You can order their blinds in custom colors, custom sizes, custom options and with custom features. All this custom makes for a tricky ordering process and a huge amount of possibility along the way.

We designed this configuration app to make it easy to build a custom blind. It provides a realistic preview of your window blind which is particularly helpful when you need to see if the fabric you've chosen is too transparent for the bathroom.

Responsive

The app needed to work on Levolor's website, store kiosks and on phones and tablets. Because the interface adapts to a variety of devices you can start a configuration in the store, preview it on your phone when you go to pick out paint and then finalize your order from your laptop at home.

Price Drawer

We mentioned there was a lot of custom right? We designed the price drawer to spin open to give you a summary of your blind and let you know if there are any conflicts with the options you've chosen.

Color Picker

In order to give people a realistic preview of their blind we needed to create a custom color picker to allow them to adjust the color of the preview room's wall and window trim. We used a combination of HTML5 canvas and CSS to optimize preformance.

Settings

Levolor sells a wide variety of window blinds that can be customized - there are thousands of possibilities! We designed a system of components that can be mixed and matched in tabs to create the perfect interface for every type of blind.
